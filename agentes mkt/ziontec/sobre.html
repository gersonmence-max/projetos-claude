Our Story

Built for New England's Clean Energy Future

We started Ziontec because we saw homeowners and businesses trying to navigate solar and EV charging separately — with different contractors, different timelines, and no one looking at the full picture.

Our Mission

One Company. Complete Power.

We believe every home and business in New England should have access to clean, independent power — without having to coordinate multiple contractors, navigate bureaucratic red tape, or figure out which rebates they qualify for.

Ziontec exists to make that simple. We install integrated solar + EV charging systems from start to finish — assessment, design, permits, installation, utility connection, and rebate filing. One company. One point of contact. Complete power.
