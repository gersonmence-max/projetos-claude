How We Calculate Your Savings

EV Charging Savings

We compare your current public charging cost per kWh vs. home charging with Eversource/National Grid off-peak EV rates (~$0.14/kWh). The difference × your monthly kWh usage = your annual savings.

Solar Savings

Based on your state's solar irradiance data and average utility rate, we estimate how much of your bill a standard rooftop system would offset — typically 70-90% in New England.

Payback Period

We use average installation costs (EV charger: ~$2,000; Solar: ~$18,000 after federal 30% ITC credit) divided by your annual savings to estimate when your system pays for itself.
